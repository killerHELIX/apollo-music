Apollo

A synchronized music player.

Listen to hand-selected tracks at the same time as others, or try your hand at the selecting.


Find Out More

Purpose

Apollo is a service meant for tabletop gamers. A story lends itself to music, and it shouldn't be a difficult process.

With Apollo, you can host rooms joinable by anyone with the invite link. The room owner can select tracks to play for everyone based on their weight and intensity. Tracks are also separated into 'loops', a smaller segment of the track that loops nicely. The owner can queue loops together to form a cohesive experience for everyone listening in.

Features

Track-synchronized rooms

Create a room for your friends that keeps track of synchronization for you.

Intuitive track selection

Select tracks with a simple UI based on their weight and momentum.

Track manipulation

Loop entire tracks indefinitely. Loop track segments indefinitely. Queue up additional loops.
